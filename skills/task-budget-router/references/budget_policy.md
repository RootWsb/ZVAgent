# Budget Policy

## Hard Defaults

- quick: at most 1 local page, 2 fresh sources, 5 bullets.
- brief: at most 3 local pages, 4 fresh sources, 1200 Chinese chars.
- deep: at most 8 local pages, 8 fresh sources before pausing to summarize.
- archive: same as deep, but write durable Markdown and update indexes.

## Expansion Rules

Expand the budget only when:

- required evidence is missing
- the user explicitly asks for depth
- the task is high stakes or current
- the initial answer must be archived or scheduled

## Stop Rules

Stop reading/searching when:

- the conclusion is adequately supported
- additional sources repeat the same claim
- source quality is lower than sources already used
- local knowledge has a good summary page and source links

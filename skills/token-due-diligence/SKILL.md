# Token Due Diligence

## Overview

Use this skill to turn a crypto project into a research memo. Separate product quality from token value capture and keep conclusions risk-first.

Prefer Chinese unless the user asks otherwise. Use `crypto-news` for current market context and `surf` for live crypto, on-chain, or project data when available.

## Workflow

1. Identify the asset precisely: project, token symbol, chain, contract if known, official docs, governance forum, explorer, and market pages.
2. Explain what the product does and what the token does. A useful product can still have weak token value capture.
3. Check tokenomics: supply, fully diluted valuation, allocation, emissions, unlocks, vesting, burn/buyback, staking rewards, and incentives.
4. Check economic evidence: users, fees, revenue, treasury, TVL when relevant, liquidity depth, retention, and competitor metrics.
5. Check control and governance: admin keys, multisig, upgradeability, voting concentration, emergency powers, and treasury control.
6. Check market structure: exchange listings, liquidity fragmentation, whale concentration, leverage/perps, airdrop farming, and regulatory exposure.
7. End with red flags, unresolved facts, and what evidence would change the research view.

## Evidence Order

- Prefer project docs, contracts, governance pages, official blog posts, audits, and treasury disclosures.
- Use structured market and DeFi data for cross-checks.
- Use specialist media and social discussion as context, not as proof.
